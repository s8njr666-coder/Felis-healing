Why This Isn't Just a Loop

1. Procedural Audio Engine: Unlike other apps that use MP3 recordings, Felis-Healing generates sound mathematically in real-time. This prevents "Ear Fatigue" by ensuring no two seconds of purring are ever identical.

2. Bio-Acoustic Frequencies: This app locks onto 25Hz - 50Hz. Scientific research shows these specific frequencies improve bone density and speed muscle repair in humans.

3. The Laryngeal Twitch: We simulate the feline brain's signal to the throat muscles (25-40 times per second), creating a tactile "gravelly" texture you can physically feel through your device.

4. Natural Breathing: The volume swells and dips to mimic a living respiratory cycle, signaling your nervous system to enter a state of deep relaxation.
